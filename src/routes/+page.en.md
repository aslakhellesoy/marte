Why teams reach for malte

---

**No selectors or ids** — mark an element and you're done.

---

**Components, not just elements** — a comment marks them, no prop types to touch.

---

**Your design stays untouched** — edit the prose, never the markup.

---

**Scoped CSS just works** — content is baked in, not injected.

---

**LLM-friendly copy** — the whole page reads top-to-bottom in one file.

---

**Fails loud** — a mismatch breaks the build instead of shipping silently.

---

**Build-time, zero runtime** — no malte code ships to the browser.

---

**Repeat with one template** — this grid is a single component, fed eight blocks.
