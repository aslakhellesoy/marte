Markdown ⇄ Svelte

---

Keep your page copy in Markdown. Keep your design in Svelte. **marte** syncs the two.

---

Why marte

---

- Edit prose in one readable place — friendly for humans *and* [LLMs](https://www.anthropic.com)
- Markers are positional: no selectors, no ids, no `:::`
- Bullets are re-skinned onto your styled `<li>` templates
- Counts are validated at build time, so a mistake fails loudly
- Works great for landing pages and marketing copy
